Last updated: 24/01/2025

This Privacy Policy applies to VoxDiary, developed and operated by EZEMP.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

However, we use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, to collect non-personal, anonymized data about app usage.

2. Firebase Analytics

Firebase Analytics may collect:

  • App usage data (screens visited, session duration)

This data is aggregated and does not personally identify users. For more details, visit Firebase Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Information

  • To monitor app performance and fix issues
  • To understand general usage patterns

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties.

Analytics data is processed by Google (Firebase) in accordance with their policies.

5. User Consent

By using VoxDiary, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

6. Data Retention

We retain anonymized analytics data only as long as necessary to improve app performance and user experience.

7. iOS Data Usage & Tracking Disclosure

VoxDiary does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

We use Firebase Analytics solely for anonymized usage insights and performance monitoring.

No data collected is linked to user identity.
